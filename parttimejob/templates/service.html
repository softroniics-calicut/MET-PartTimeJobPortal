Catering

Catering involves providing food for events or gatherings. This includes menu planning, preparation, delivery, and sometimes on-site serving. Caterers tailor their offerings to meet the specific needs and preferences of clients, ranging from casual to formal occasions.

Shop Keeping

Shopkeeping involves managing a retail business, including tasks like inventory control, customer service, sales, and maintaining the overall store environment. Successful shopkeepers often focus on providing a positive customer experience and efficient operations to drive sales and build customer loyalty.
